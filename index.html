Enter the retail revolution. revolution.
Delfrez, a chain of state-of-the-art stores from the house of Suguna Foods, was started in 2021 to provide healthy processed meat to our customers. The meat is high in nutritional value and brings a lip-smacking flavour to relish our consumers’ taste buds. Our processing plants are designed on par with international standards that ensure freshness, quality, and hygiene. With a primary focus on food safety, the plants are validated with FSSC 22000 accreditation and use HACCP – Hazard Analysis Critical Control Point.
Delfrez brings various meat products in chicken, mutton, eggs, ready-to-eat, and ready-to-cook categories (marinades), in modern and general trade. All our products are available in Delfrez stores and leading supermarkets near you, and customers can choose desired portions & cuts of their favourite meat.
